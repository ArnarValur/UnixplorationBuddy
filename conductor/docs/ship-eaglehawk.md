## Design Notes

- **Role:** Deep-space exploration, exobiology, colonisation scouting

- **Key engineering:** FSD for range, Thrusters for planetary landings, DSS for mapping efficiency
- **Guardian Booster:** +10.5 ly boost from 5H booster
- **Anti-Corrosion Cargo:** For Thargoid/Guardian artefact recovery
- **AFMU:** Self-repair in the black, no station needed
- **Colonisation Suite:** Ready for colony drops in the field
